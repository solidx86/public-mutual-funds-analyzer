Investment Strategy

Starting Simple: You have 4 funds. Check your portfolio monthly — read the PIATAF and PAIF NAV, check if either has dropped ≥10% from their recent high, and decide whether to top up from your PMMF-A reserve. That is the entire active management required.

Entry & DCA / RSP Plan

  • Lump sum entry: RM 1,000 → PIATAF RM 550 · PAIF RM 200 · PeEMAS RM 100 · PMMF-A RM 150
  • Monthly RSP: RM 200–500/month; split PIATAF 55% / PAIF 25% / PMMF-A 20%
  • Gold top-up: PeEMAS once every 3 months — slow-moving hedge, not a trading vehicle
  • Cooling-off right: As first-time investor you have 6 business days to withdraw without sales charge

Tactical Dip Playbook

  • Trigger: PIATAF or PAIF drops ≥10% from recent high (PIATAF ATH: 0.9506 · PAIF ATH: 0.6503)
  • Action: Redeem RM 50–100 from PMMF-A → switch into the dipping fund
  • Max per dip: Keep minimum RM 50 in PMMF-A at all times
  • Replenish: Increase next 2 months' RSP to PMMF-A to rebuild the reserve
  • Never: Do not panic-sell equity funds during corrections

Distribution Policy

  • PIATAF, PAIF, PeEMAS: Incidental — reinvest all payouts immediately
  • PMMF-A: Annual — reinvest to compound inside money market
  • Growth mandate = growth over income; do not switch to income funds before age 50
  • Reinvested distributions are tax-exempt for Malaysian individuals — compounding is free

Rebalancing & Review

  • Annual review: Rebalance if PIATAF drifts above 65% or PMMF-A falls below 10%
  • Alpha watchlist: Replace PAIF if 5Y alpha drops to zero or beat rate falls below 75%
  • Gold review: Re-evaluate PeEMAS only if gold enters a confirmed multi-year bear market
  • Portfolio expansion: After 12 months, consider adding a Malaysia domestic fund as a 5th position using review cycle profits
