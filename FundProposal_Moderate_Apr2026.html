Investment Strategy

Dollar Cost Averaging / Regular Savings Plan (RSP)

Set up monthly RSP across all 6 funds at the allocated ratios. Ringgit cost averaging means you automatically buy more units when prices are low and fewer when prices are high — smoothing out your entry points across market cycles.

Analysts expect KLCI softness in H1 2026 (geopolitical headwinds) before H2 recovery. Your monthly RSP automatically takes advantage of lower prices during this period. Consider additional lump-sum top-ups during market corrections of 10% or more.

Distribution Policy

Reinvest all distributions. Unit trust distributions to individual Malaysian investors are tax-exempt. By reinvesting, you harness the power of compounding — your distributions buy more units, which generate more distributions, creating a virtuous cycle. Over a 5–10 year horizon, compounding makes a significant difference.

Rebalancing Triggers

  • Time-based: Review portfolio every 6 months
  • Drift-based: Rebalance when any fund category drifts more than 10% from its target allocation
  • Life event: Major changes (marriage, children, property purchase, job change) warrant a fresh suitability review
  • Market event: If broad market corrects more than 15%, consider it an opportunity to top up equity positions
  • New data: When the monthly fund screener produces updated qualification results, review if any recommended funds have been disqualified
