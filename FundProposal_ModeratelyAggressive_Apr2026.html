💰 Distribution Policy

Reinvest all distributions. Compounding is tax-exempt for Malaysian individuals — taking payouts is voluntarily forfeiting the most powerful force in long-term investing. PITSEQ and PSSF historically reinvest into units, amplifying NAV growth compounding over time.

⚖️ Rebalancing Triggers
  • Time-based: Every 6 months — review allocation drift
  • Drift-based: Rebalance when any fund drifts >10% from target
  • Event-based: Major life changes, BNM rate shift, significant market correction
  • Next milestone: October 2026 — assess adding a 5th fund (Mixed Asset or Regional Equity)
🌱 New Investor Foundation
  • Cooling-off right: 6 business days from date of first investment — no penalty to exit within this window. Use this time to confirm your comfort with the portfolio.
  • Recommended approach: Start with a modest initial lump sum to experience live NAV movements firsthand. Scale up RSP commitment after the first month once you are comfortable with how unit trust NAVs behave.
  • Risk reminder: Both PITSEQ (−5.58%) and PSSF (−6.14%) are near their all-time highs. If a market correction occurs shortly after investing, hold firm — this is normal and expected for Risk Level 4 equity funds. The data shows these managers have consistently recovered and created alpha over 3Y and 5Y.
  • 6-month review: After gaining comfort, discuss adding a Mixed Asset or Asia/Global Equity fund as portfolio slot #5 to normalise equity allocation and add geographic diversification.
