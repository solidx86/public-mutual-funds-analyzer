Investment Strategy

1. Regular Savings Plan (RSP)

Monthly RSP split across the two balanced sleeves and the sukuk fund in proportion to their allocations. Add lump-sum top-ups when any sleeve corrects 10%+ from its all-time high.

2. Distribution Policy

Reinvest all distributions — compounding is tax-exempt for Malaysian individuals. The sukuk sleeve distributes income periodically; reinvesting it is what turns carry into growth.

3. Rebalancing Triggers

Review every 6 months; rebalance when any sleeve drifts more than 10% from target, or on major life events.

4. Tactical Dip-Capture Playbook

When any equity or balanced sleeve falls 10% or more from its all-time high, switch from PeCDF-A into that sleeve — deploying at most 50% of the money market reserve per dip. Replenish the reserve over the next 2–3 months of RSP before the next deployment.
