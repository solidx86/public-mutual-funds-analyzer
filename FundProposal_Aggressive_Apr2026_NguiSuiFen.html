Rebalancing Triggers
  1. Time-based: Portfolio review every quarter (Aggressive profile). Check allocation drift and fund performance.

Tactical Dip Capture Playbook

How to Use the 12% PeCDF-A Reserve

  • Step 1 — Watch: Check PIATAF and PISTF ATH Drawdown % monthly (available on FundMaster)
